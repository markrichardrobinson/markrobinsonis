ABOUT THE WORK

The work on this site grows slowly. It begins in sermons preached to real congregations, in fragments caught on buses, and in conversations in hospital waiting rooms and on street corners.

Drawings come from the same place: loose graphite lines, simple shapes, and a limited palette. Each gesture carries weight — a hand, a boat, a pair of shoes, a stretch of railway line.

These books and images are created to be handled, argued with, prayed over, and shared. They belong with people who care about justice, healing, and the daily labour of hope.
