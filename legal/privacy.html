Privacy statement

How this site handles your information.

This is a small, personal site. It does not exist to track you, profile you, or sell your details. Any information you choose to share is treated with care.

Website data

This site is built as a simple HTML/CSS website. It does not directly collect analytics data or use invasive tracking cookies.

Your web browser and internet service provider may log standard technical information (such as IP address and pages visited) as part of how the internet works. That data is handled under their own policies.

Contact details

If you contact me by email, I will see the information you choose to share: your email address, your name if you include it, and the contents of your message.

I use this information only to respond to you, and to keep basic records of correspondence where that is pastorally or practically necessary. Your details are not sold or passed on for marketing.

Mailing and updates

If you ask to be kept informed about future work (for example, the Pulse devotional), your email address may be kept in a simple list for that purpose. You can ask to be removed at any time.

Questions

If you have any questions or concerns about how your information is handled, you can contact me at hello@markrobinsonis.com .
